Runze Zhang

I’m a Milnor Lecturer at Stony Brook University. I earned my PhD from Université Paul-Sabatier in Toulouse under the supervision of Pascale Roesch. My research focuses on holomorphic dynamics of dimension one.

Research

My research investigates the structure of parameter spaces for cubic polynomials, with an emphasis on parabolic and Siegel dynamics.

Publications

  • "On Dynamical Parameter Space of Cubic Polynomials with a Parabolic Fixed Point," J. Lond. Math. Soc., Volume 110, Issue 6, 2024.
  • "Rigidity of Bounded Type Cubic Siegel Polynomials," arXiv:2311.00431.
  • "Parabolic Components in Cubic Polynomial Slice Per 1 (1)," arXiv:2210.14305.
